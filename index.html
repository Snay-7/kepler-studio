The thesis

The companies that will define the next decade build their own software.

Off-the-shelf tools are levellers. They give every business in your category access to the same workflows, the same dashboards, the same constraints. They make you average by design.

The teams pulling ahead — in finance, real estate, life sciences, e-commerce, legal, healthcare — are doing the opposite. They're building software shaped around how they actually operate. Custom platforms. AI agents that handle the work. Integrated, not bolted-on.

Kleper is the studio they bring in to build it.

Most agencies talk about AI agents. We built one end-to-end. 3,000 lines of production Python across three independent stages — sourcer, auditor, pitch generator — that turn a list of business URLs into client-ready analysis briefs.

Common questions, straight answers.

Don't see yours? Email hello@kleper.studio and we'll reply within a working day.

What kind of projects do you take on?
AI agents, custom platforms, websites, and AI-native software for businesses that have outgrown off-the-shelf tools. We work across finance, real estate, life sciences, e-commerce, legal, healthcare, hospitality and consumer brands. The industry varies. The standard doesn't.
How much does an engagement cost?
It depends entirely on scope. A focused website project starts in the low thousands. A custom platform with AI integration runs into five figures and beyond. Every engagement gets a fixed-price proposal after a discovery conversation — no surprise invoices, no scope creep.
How long does a build take?
Most engagements run six to twelve weeks. Larger platforms run longer. We commit to dates in the proposal and we hit them — Valora was built end-to-end in six weeks.
Do I own the code, the data, and the AI models?
Yes — all three. Code lives in a Git repo we hand over to you. Data lives in databases you control. AI prompts, fine-tunes, and configurations are documented and yours from day one. If you ever leave Kleper, you walk out with everything.
Can you integrate with what we already have?
Almost certainly. Payments, accounting, CRM, marketing, support, scheduling, custom APIs, legacy databases — if it has an API, we can connect it. If it doesn't, we'll build one.
Why not just hire someone in-house?
Because one person can't be a strategist, a designer, a frontend engineer, a backend engineer, and an AI specialist. Kleper gives you that team — and the systems we've already built — for less than the cost of one senior in-house hire.
